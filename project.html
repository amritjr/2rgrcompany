The Gurkhas are a unique unit in the Army with a reputation of being amongst the finest and most feared soldiers in the world.
The Royal Gurkha Rifles are Infantry Soldiers and Officers.
The role of the Infantry is at the core of the Army; from peacekeeping to combat operations, anywhere in the world - our Infanteers lead the way.
Ready to operate alongside allies in adverse climates, from desert to jungle. Ready to protect the nation, prevent conflict,
fight the nation's enemies and deal with disaster at home or overseas.
